About

All about me


I'm Sasawat Yimleang, a computer programming and analysis student at Seneca College in Ontario. Originally from Thailand, I am enthusiastic about embracing new challenges and learning through continuous growth.

I hold a degree in Mechanical Engineering from Thailand, which has equipped me with a strong foundation in analytical thinking and problem-solving. Currently, I am expanding my knowledge and honing my programming skills at Seneca College, where I'm specializing in computer programming and analysis.

My journey in the world of puzzles began with a fascination for Sudoku, a captivating number puzzle that has improved my logical thinking and problem-solving skills. As a mechanical engineer, I appreciated the precision and order that Sudoku demanded, but my love for logical puzzles led me to explore computer programming and analysis as a new career path.
